# Financing Triad Framework (融资三级分类)

Categorize every HK IPO by **Net Fundraising Amount (净融资额)**:

## Classification Matrix

| Category | Threshold | Risk | Action |
|----------|-----------|------|--------|
| **All-in** | > 1B HKD | Low | Max Margin |
| **Death Zone** | 200M - 500M HKD | HIGH | HARD PASS |
| **Casino** | < 200M HKD | Volatile | Speculative |

---

## 1. All-in (众星拱月)

**Threshold**: > 1 Billion HKD

**Context**:
- "New Economy" giants or Unicorns
- Top-tier PE/VC seeking USD exit
- Strong cornerstone (基石) support
- Green Shoe protection expected

**Logic**: "Safety in Numbers" - Massive size implies institutional backing.

**Action**: **STRONGLY RECOMMEND**
- Strategy: Max Margin / 乙组
- Expected: Even if Day 1 flat, sponsor protection limits downside

**Examples**: SF Express (6936.HK) ~6B HKD, Mixue (蜜雪冰城)

---

## 2. Death Zone (死亡地带)

**Threshold**: 200 Million - 500 Million HKD

**Context**: The MOST DANGEROUS category.
- Too small for institutional coverage (no Green Shoe power)
- Too large for 庄家 to fully control
- Usually traditional industries (Property, Manufacturing)
- Ineligible for Stock Connect (港股通) - no passive fund inflow

**Logic**: "Liquidity Trap" - Retail investors get stuck here.

**Action**: **AVOID / HARD PASS** - This is the core negative filter.

**Warning Signs**:
- Subscription < 15x
- No cornerstone investors
- Traditional/boring sector

---

## 3. Casino (庄股/围飞)

**Threshold**: < 200 Million HKD

**Context**:
- Highly manipulated "Shell Games" (围飞)
- If Dealer is strong (强庄), can pump +200% on Day 1
- High variance: either moon or crash

**Action**: **SPECULATIVE BET** (only if signals align)

**Entry Conditions** (must meet at least one):
- Margin Ratio > 20x (Retail FOMO signal)
- Top 25 shareholders hold > 90% (货源归边)
- Sponsor has P&D history

**Exit Strategy**: Hit and Run
- Sell at Day 1 Open or Dark Pool High
- NEVER hold overnight

**Classic Case**: 鲁大师 (3601.HK) - Day 1 +218%
