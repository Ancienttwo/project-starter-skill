# Sponsor Analysis (保荐人分析)

> "保荐人决定了是有绿鞋还是只有陷阱。"

Sponsor quality acts as a **multiplier** on the Financing Triad classification.

## Sponsor Tier Classification

### Tier 1: Kingmakers / Ruthless Traders

**Members**: Goldman Sachs, Morgan Stanley, JPMorgan, CICC (中金)

**Characteristics**:
- Strong execution muscle
- Will "eat the Green Shoe" if project is weak
- Dual-edged sword: protection OR exploitation

**Signal Interpretation**:
| Scenario | Signal |
|----------|--------|
| Subscription > 50x | 🟢 BULLISH - sponsors will protect |
| Subscription < 15x | 🔴 CAUTION - Green Shoe Trap likely |

**Green Shoe Trap Mechanism**:
1. Sponsor holds short position via Green Shoe
2. If subscription weak, they let price drop on Day 1
3. Buy back shares at low price to close short
4. Profit from spread while retail loses

**Classic Case**: 奈雪的茶 (2150.HK)
- JPM + CMB sponsorship, 200x subscription
- Priced at upper range (overvalued)
- Day 1: **-13%** despite "hot" status
- Lesson: Tier 1 + extreme valuation = short risk

---

### Tier 2: Standard / Neutral

**Members**: CLSA, Haitong (海通), Huatai (华泰), Guotai Junan (国泰君安)

**Characteristics**:
- Neutral execution
- Neither strong protection nor exploitation
- Follow market sentiment

**Signal**: NEUTRAL - base decision on other factors

---

### Tier 3: Shell Makers / 围飞专业户

**Members**: Small local brokerages (e.g., Great Roc, Lego, Fortune Financial)

**Characteristics**:
- Shell game specialists
- Coordinated pump-and-dump potential
- High variance outcomes

**Signal Interpretation**:
| Scenario | Outcome |
|----------|---------|
| Strong dealer control | +200% pump possible |
| Weak control | -50% dump likely |

**Entry Criteria** (Casino category only):
- Check dealer's track record
- Look for 货源归边 signals
- Must have exit plan (Day 1 only)

## Combined Analysis Matrix

| Sponsor | + Large Cap (>1B) | + Death Zone | + Small Cap (<200M) |
|---------|-------------------|--------------|---------------------|
| **Tier 1** | 🟢 CONFIRMED ALL-IN | 🔴 Avoid | 🔴 Avoid |
| **Tier 2** | 🟢 Standard ALL-IN | 🔴 Avoid | 🟡 Caution |
| **Tier 3** | 🟡 Unusual (verify) | 🔴 Avoid | 🟡 SHELL GAME POTENTIAL |

---

## Red Flags

1. **Tier 1 + Cold Stock**: Green Shoe Trap confirmed
2. **Tier 1 + Priced at Upper Range**: Overvaluation risk
3. **Tier 3 + Scattered Shareholding**: No dealer control
4. **Any Tier + Death Zone**: Universal AVOID

## Green Flags

1. **Tier 1 + Hot Sector + 50x+ Subscription**: Maximum confidence
2. **Tier 3 + Concentrated Holding (>90%)**: Shell game upside
3. **Multiple Tier 1 Co-sponsors**: Exceptional project
